Spin It!

- A mobile leaderboard game where you spin (swipe) a 3d cube according to the spin direction on screen on a time limit with the goal of reaching a high score and a high round count in the leaderboards.

* Created using Hybrid app technologies: Ionic 3, Angular 5 and a Cordova wrapping.
* Integrated with Git, Google Play Games Services, Billing & Purchases, Analytics and Ads.
* Available on Google Play:
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=markm.spinit
